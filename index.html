Our background

With nearly 20 years of experience in software development, we've collaborated with enterprises and startups across the US, Singapore and New Zealand, including industry leaders like Twitter and IBM.

Our services

Advise

  • We provide expert consultation to refine your ideas and guide your projects. From understanding your target audience to transforming your vision into actionable solutions, we’re here to help every step of the way.
  • We offer guidance on advancing your project, including selecting the right project management methodologies, best practices, tools, services, and technology stacks.
  • We conduct research and requirement analysis to define how your product should function. Additionally, we can create prototypes or MVPs, giving you a tangible preview of your product’s look and feel.

Develop

  • We can gather your product requirements and create a detailed technical roadmap to guide its development.
  • We will define the scope, design, and propose technical solutions for your project.
  • Our team can assist in building and managing your technical team, using modern software development methodologies to ensure your product stays on track.
  • We specialize in both backend and frontend development, as well as integrating your product with other systems and services.

Operate

  • We provide comprehensive support for launching your product on cloud platforms.
  • From infrastructure to DevOps, we handle it all.
  • Our team monitors application performance, identifies and resolves errors, and ensures robust security.
  • Additionally, we offer optimization and scalability solutions to ensure your product aligns seamlessly with customer needs.
